## Specs (for Google rich results)

- **Format:** JPG or PNG (JPG preferred for smaller file size)
- **Dimensions:** 512×512 pixels minimum, square aspect ratio (1:1)
- **File size:** Under 200KB
- **Subject:** Clear face, professional headshot, neutral or branded background
- **Privacy:** Do NOT use stock photos for real people — Google may penalize fake author identity

## What happens if photo is missing?

Falls back to `ui-avatars.com` — generates an orange circle with the author's initials. This works but won't help SEO.
